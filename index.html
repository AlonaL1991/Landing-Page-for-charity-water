Our Mission

charity: water is a nonprofit organization bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. Our mission is to end the water crisis and change lives by helping communities access clean water through sustainable projects. We believe that everyone deserves clean water, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality.
